## Overview

Arxtect is an AI-powered LaTeX editor for researchers. Dianne led product design and brand strategy, delivering a marketing website redesign and establishing the design system foundation for the internal editor interface.

## The Challenge

The existing product had strong engineering but weak visual communication. Researchers couldn't immediately understand the product's value, and the interface felt disconnected from the brand.

## Approach

### 01. Bridging Design & Engineering
Working directly with the engineering team, Dianne translated design specs into buildable components, reducing back-and-forth and shipping faster.

### 02. Visual De-risking for Complex Tools
For a tool targeting researchers, cognitive load is everything. The redesign stripped unnecessary visual complexity while preserving all functionality.

### 03. Strategic Content Reframing
The marketing site was restructured around researcher pain points, not feature lists.

## Outcome

A cohesive product identity, a marketing site that communicates value immediately, and a design system the team can build on.
