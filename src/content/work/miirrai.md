## Overview

Miirr.ai is an AI fashion platform. Dianne led UX design and prototype development from concept to investor-ready demo, establishing the product's strategic UX foundation for launch.

## The Challenge

Tight timeline, zero existing design system, and a product that needed to communicate trust to both fashion-forward users and investors.

## Approach

### 01. Domain Expertise as UX Strategy
Understanding fashion culture informed every design decision — from terminology to visual hierarchy.

### 02. Hybrid Prototyping for Speed
Combining low-fi flows with high-fi key screens allowed rapid iteration without sacrificing presentation quality.

### 03. Visuals that Build Trust
For an AI product in a skeptical industry, the visual language needed to feel premium, not experimental.

## Outcome

Investor-ready prototype delivered on schedule, with two complete user flows and a scalable design foundation.
