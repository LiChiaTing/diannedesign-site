Dianne is a multidisciplinary designer with roots in film, a detour through dance, and a deep belief that the best design feels obvious — after the fact. Dianne 是一位跨領域設計師，根植於電影，繞道過舞蹈，深信最好的設計在事後看來顯而易見。

The Story 故事

Dianne grew up in Taiwan, surrounded by a culture that valued both discipline and creativity. She studied Film and Graphic Design — learning early that storytelling is everything, and that the gap between a good idea and a great one is almost always visual.

Dianne 在台灣長大，在一個既重視紀律也珍視創意的文化中成長。她攻讀電影與平面設計，很早就明白：說故事是一切的核心，而好作品與偉大作品之間的距離，幾乎永遠是視覺。

For three years, Dianne co-founded and ran a swing dance studio in Taiwan. She taught classes, ran campaigns that grew enrollment by 150%, and managed a small creative team — all while learning that community is a design problem too.

三年間，Dianne 在台灣共同創立並經營一間搖擺舞蹈工作室。她教授課程、策劃讓學生人數成長 150% 的行銷活動，管理小型創意團隊——同時學到，社群本身也是一個設計問題。

The film background never left. It shapes how Dianne approaches pacing in user flows, visual hierarchy in layouts, and the kind of emotional arc a good onboarding should have. Design is storytelling — just with fewer jump cuts.

電影背景從未離開她。它影響著 Dianne 看待使用者流程節奏的方式、版面視覺層次的直覺，以及好的 onboarding 應有的情感弧線。設計就是說故事——只是少了幾個跳接鏡頭。

A Master's in User Experience brought Dianne to the Pacific Northwest. She now designs for AI-powered products, works across research, interaction, and visual design — and builds her portfolio alongside Claude.

使用者體驗碩士學位把 Dianne 帶到了太平洋西北岸。她現在為 AI 驅動的產品做設計，跨足研究、互動與視覺設計——並且和 Claude 一起建造她的作品集。

For me, design isn't just about interfaces. It's about removing friction, creating clarity, and sparking delight. I love when something I create helps people get from "this is complicated" to "oh, that was easy." 對我來說，設計不只是介面。而是消除摩擦、創造清晰，以及帶來那一瞬間的驚喜。我最喜歡的，就是讓人從「這好複雜」走到「原來這麼簡單」。
